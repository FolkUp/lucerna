Lucerna offers selective OSINT research services to organizations and individuals who need rigorous, evidence-based intelligence. Every engagement follows our published methodology — transparent sources, reproducible analysis, graded confidence.

We work with law firms, compliance departments, journalists, and investors.

## OSINT Due Diligence

Background checks on companies, individuals, and transactions using exclusively open sources. Corporate structure mapping, beneficial ownership tracing, reputation analysis, digital footprint assessment.

Typical scope: counterparty verification, pre-investment screening, M&A target profiling.

## Fact-Check & Verification

Independent verification of specific claims, statements, or published information. Source triangulation, timeline reconstruction, metadata analysis.

Typical scope: media claims verification, witness statement cross-referencing, document authenticity assessment.

## Compliance Research

Regulatory analysis for organizations operating across complex jurisdictions. EU AI Act readiness, GDPR compliance auditing, cross-border data transfer assessments.

Typical scope: regulatory gap analysis, compliance documentation review, policy recommendations.
