## What is Lucerna?

**Lucerna** (Latin: *lantern*) is FolkUp's open research lab. We publish OSINT investigations, fact-checks, and methodological guides — all freely available, all open to scrutiny.

## Why "Lucerna"?

A lucerna was an oil lamp in ancient Rome — a small source of light in the dark. Our goal is similar: to illuminate claims, verify facts, and make our research process transparent.

## Principles

1. **Transparency** — every investigation discloses its methodology
2. **Source-first** — claims are only as strong as their sources
3. **Open access** — all content is free and licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0
4. **No advocacy** — we present findings, not opinions

## Who we are

Lucerna is a project by [FolkUp Research Lab](https://folkup.app) — an independent research collective based in Europe. We don't put names on bylines. Our work speaks for itself.
