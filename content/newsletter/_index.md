Stay current with open-source intelligence research. The OSINT Digest delivers curated content from Lucerna's investigations and the broader OSINT community — directly to your inbox, every two weeks.

## What you get

- **Case study breakdowns** — how we conduct investigations, step by step
- **Tool reviews** — practical assessments of OSINT tools, with real-world examples
- **Methodology insights** — source evaluation techniques, verification frameworks, analytical tradecraft
- **Industry signals** — regulatory changes, new data sources, emerging threats

## Who reads it

Compliance officers, investigative journalists, legal researchers, due diligence analysts, and anyone who works with open sources professionally.

## Our standards

Every issue follows the same principles as our published research: transparent sourcing, graded confidence, no speculation. We do not share, sell, or otherwise distribute subscriber data. See our [Privacy Policy]({{< relref "/legal/privacy-policy" >}}).

You can unsubscribe at any time — every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link.
