## Cookie-Free Declaration

**This website does not use cookies.**

Lucerna is designed to operate without any cookies, including:

- **No analytics cookies** — we do not use cookie-based analytics
- **No tracking cookies** — we do not track users across sessions or websites
- **No advertising cookies** — we do not display advertising
- **No functional cookies** — the website operates without storing cookies in your browser
- **No third-party cookies** — no external services set cookies through our site

## Analytics

Analytics are currently **disabled** on this website. When re-enabled, any analytics solution will require your explicit consent before activation, in accordance with ePrivacy Directive Art. 5(3) and EDPB Guidelines 2/2023.

## Local Storage

The Blowfish theme may use the browser's `localStorage` to remember your preferences (such as dark/light mode selection and font size). This data:
- Stays on your device and is never sent to our servers
- Can be cleared at any time through your browser settings

Under EDPB Guidelines 2/2023, access to information stored on terminal equipment (including `localStorage`) falls within the scope of ePrivacy Directive Art. 5(3). We rely on the strictly necessary exemption for this functionality, as it is required to provide a service explicitly requested by the user (theme preference).

## Third-Party Services

If you interact with third-party services linked from our site (such as Ko-fi or GitHub), those services may set their own cookies. Please refer to their respective cookie policies.
