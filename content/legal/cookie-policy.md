## Cookie-Free Declaration

**This website does not use cookies.**

We have designed Lucerna to operate without any cookies, including:

- **No analytics cookies** — our analytics solution (Umami) is cookie-free and privacy-focused
- **No tracking cookies** — we do not track users across sessions or websites
- **No advertising cookies** — we do not display advertising
- **No functional cookies** — the website operates without storing data in your browser
- **No third-party cookies** — no external services set cookies through our site

## Local Storage

The Blowfish theme may use the browser's `localStorage` to remember your preferences (such as dark/light mode selection and font size). This data:
- Stays on your device and is never sent to our servers
- Can be cleared at any time through your browser settings
- Is not considered a "cookie" under ePrivacy regulations

## Third-Party Services

If you interact with third-party services linked from our site (such as Ko-fi or GitHub), those services may set their own cookies. Please refer to their respective cookie policies.
