Lucerna publishes open-source intelligence research across three domains. Every finding is documented with primary sources, methodology notes, and confidence assessments.

## [Investigations]({{< relref "/investigations" >}})

Deep-dive OSINT investigations into corporate structures, individuals, and claims. Multi-source analysis with evidence grading.

## [Studies]({{< relref "/studies" >}})

Analytical research on technology, regulation, and industry trends. Data-driven, with reproducible methodology.

## [Fact-Checks]({{< relref "/verifications" >}})

Verification of specific claims using open sources. Each claim assessed independently with a confidence rating.
