## Content Strategy for Mastodon

#### Daily (1-2 posts)
- **Quick insights** from ongoing research
- **Tool tips** and OSINT methodology notes
- **Regulatory updates** affecting Portugal/EU
- **Boosts** of quality community content

#### Weekly (2-3 dedicated posts)
- **Thread series** on research methodology
- **Case study** analysis (anonymized)
- **Tool reviews** and comparisons
- **Community questions** and discussions

#### Monthly (1-2 major posts)
- **Methodology deep dives**
- **Research report** summaries
- **Community collaboration** announcements
- **Educational content** series

### Mastodon-Specific Features Usage

#### Hashtags Strategy
- **Always use**: #OSINT #Research
- **Regular rotation**: #Portugal #Transparency #OpenAccess #Privacy #LegalTech #Compliance
- **Trending participation**: Check trending hashtags for relevant discussions
- **Community tags**: #InfoSec #OpenData #DigitalRights #EUCompliance

#### Content Warning Usage
- **Legal sensitive content**: Use CW for potentially sensitive legal discussions
- **Research methods**: CW for advanced/complex methodology discussions
- **Data visualization**: CW for complex charts/data that might be overwhelming

#### Threading Strategy
- **Methodology explanations**: Break complex topics into digestible thread segments
- **Case studies**: Use threads for step-by-step analysis walkthrough
- **Tool tutorials**: Multi-post threads with screenshots and explanations
- **Regulatory analysis**: Threads for complex legal framework explanations

## Engagement Strategy

### Following Strategy
- **OSINT practitioners**: Established researchers and analysts
- **Privacy advocates**: Digital rights organizations and activists
- **Academic researchers**: University-based researchers in relevant fields
- **Legal tech community**: Legal technology developers and users
- **Portuguese tech community**: Local technology and research professionals
- **EU policy watchers**: Analysts following EU regulatory developments

### Daily Engagement Activities (10-15 mins)
1. **Check notifications** and respond to mentions/replies
2. **Boost 2-3 quality posts** from research community
3. **Reply thoughtfully** to 1-2 discussion threads
4. **Share quick insight** or tip from daily work
5. **Monitor hashtags** for relevant discussions

### Weekly Engagement Activities (30 mins)
1. **Start discussion thread** on methodology or tools
2. **Share detailed analysis** of recent regulatory development
3. **Engage with Portuguese** Mastodon community
4. **Cross-post** selected LinkedIn content (adapted for platform)
5. **Follow 5-10 new relevant accounts**

### Community Building Tactics
- **Ask questions** to encourage community discussion
- **Share failures and lessons learned** for authenticity
- **Offer help** with Portugal-specific research questions
- **Collaborate publicly** on community projects
- **Tag relevant experts** in discussions (respectfully)

## Privacy and Security Considerations

### Account Security
- **Two-factor authentication**: Enable for account protection
- **Strong password**: Use password manager generated password
- **Recovery options**: Secure backup codes storage
- **Regular security review**: Monthly account security check

### Content Privacy
- **No personal information**: Keep all content professional
- **Anonymize case studies**: Remove identifying information
- **Secure research practices**: Don't expose ongoing investigations
- **Source protection**: Never compromise source confidentiality

### Instance Selection Security
- **Server location**: Prefer EU-based instances for GDPR compliance
- **Moderation policies**: Review and ensure alignment with values
- **Federation policies**: Understand which instances are federated/blocked
- **Backup plan**: Have account migration plan if needed
