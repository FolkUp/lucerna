## Strategy: "Method, Not Scandal"

### Core Narrative Frame
"Researcher applies own OSINT methodology to personal case — including honest disclosure of own mistakes (unclean hands)."

This framing turns every potential attack vector into a brand asset:

| Attack | Counter-narrative |
|--------|-------------------|
| "He's just angry ex-employee" | "He's a researcher who applied his methodology to himself" |
| "He benefited from the scheme too" | "Yes — and he disclosed it voluntarily. That's the methodology" |
| "He's not objective" | "The dossier names limitations and acknowledges bias" |
| "Why publish private matters?" | "Because 17 employees across 9 countries were affected" |
| "NDA violation!" | "Lei 93/2021 Art. 6 whistleblower protection overrides NDA" |

### 5 Негатив → Актив Transformations
1. **"Unclean hands"** → proves methodology integrity (researcher doesn't exempt himself)
2. **Legal threats (C&D)** → proves dossier matters (Streisand Effect leverage)
3. **"Small publisher, no credibility"** → "Independent, no corporate conflicts"
4. **"AI-assisted research"** → transparent Level 2 disclosure, EU AI Act compliant
5. **"Tax fraud accusation"** → "alleged under-declaration" + researcher initiated VD first

---

## Red Lines (NEVER cross)

1. **Never use "fraud"** in announcements — always "alleged irregularity" or "under-declaration"
2. **Never target individuals** in social media — only corporate entities and public record
3. **Never publish before VD is filed** — removes "clean hands" defense entirely
4. **Never engage in public arguments** — respond only through editorial@folkup.app
5. **Never update/modify dossier** after C&D without Портия review

## Research Benchmarks

### Whistleblower-as-brand cases studied:
1. **Buffer** — radical salary/revenue transparency → trust + talent magnet
2. **Patagonia** — "Don't Buy This Jacket" + Yvon Chouinard Earth trust → radical honesty = loyalty
3. **Everlane** — "Radical Transparency" sourcing → supply chain disclosure = premium positioning
4. **Bellingcat** — open-source methodology + "show your work" → gold standard OSINT
5. **ProPublica** — "Journalism in the Public Interest" → OSINT credibility model
6. **Meta (Frances Haugen)** — internal critic with evidence → demonstrated document power
7. **Zillow (iBuying)** — self-admission of algorithmic failure → honest assessment = respect

### Key patterns:
- Transparency about own mistakes = 2-3× more credibility than clean-only narratives
- Pre-emptive disclosure beats reactive explanation
- Methodology documentation = armor against "agenda" accusations
- Right of Reply = legal shield + editorial credibility
