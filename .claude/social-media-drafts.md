**About (1847/2000 chars):**

Lucerna is FolkUp's independent research laboratory focused on open-source intelligence (OSINT) investigations and transparent fact-checking.

We investigate facts hidden in plain sight using publicly available sources. Our work ranges from verifying claims in public discourse to documenting methodologies that others can replicate.

What we do:
- Conduct OSINT investigations on matters of public interest
- Publish transparent fact-checks with full source citations
- Document research methodologies and analytical frameworks
- Maintain open-access case files under CC BY-SA 4.0 license

Our principles:
- Source-first approach: every claim traceable to primary evidence
- Transparency: methodology and limitations stated explicitly
- Open access: research available without paywalls
- No advocacy: we document facts, not positions

The name "Lucerna" (Latin: oil lamp) reflects our purpose — illuminating what lies in shadows through systematic research.

All investigations published at lucerna.folkup.app are available under Creative Commons licensing. We operate as a boutique research unit, not a commercial intelligence service.
