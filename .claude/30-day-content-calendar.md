## Content Creation Guidelines

### LinkedIn Content Standards
- **Length**: 1,300-1,500 characters for optimal engagement
- **Images**: High-quality infographics, charts, or branded graphics
- **CTAs**: Clear call-to-action in every post
- **Professional tone**: Authoritative but approachable
- **Value-first**: Always provide actionable insights

### Mastodon Content Standards
- **Length**: Flexible, use threads for longer content
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant hashtags per post
- **Community focus**: Encourage discussion and collaboration
- **Authentic tone**: More casual and behind-the-scenes
- **Engagement**: Ask questions, respond to comments

### Visual Content Requirements
- **Brand consistency**: Lucerna colors and fonts
- **Professional quality**: Clean, readable graphics
- **Information density**: High value-to-space ratio
- **Accessibility**: Alt-text for all images
- **Mobile optimization**: Readable on small screens

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Actions (15 minutes)
1. **Check notifications** on both platforms
2. **Respond to comments** within 4 hours during business hours
3. **Like and comment** on 3-5 relevant posts
4. **Share 1 piece** of valuable external content with commentary

### Weekly Actions (30 minutes)
1. **Review analytics** and adjust strategy as needed
2. **Plan next week's content** based on performance
3. **Engage with Portuguese business community** posts
4. **Connect with 5-10 new relevant professionals**

### Monthly Actions (1 hour)
1. **Comprehensive analytics review**
2. **Content calendar planning** for next month
3. **Community feedback integration**
4. **Strategy refinement** based on results
